GE Rules

  • Students are advised to read all the instructions carefully.

  • One student can apply only for one GE Subject.

  • Students cannot apply for the subject offered by their own Department; they have to apply for Only One GE Subject offered by other 4 Departments.

  • Admission Process will be carried out on First Come First Serve Basis by system.

  • Intake Capacity for Each General Elective Subject is 120.

  • Once 120 seats get filled, the respective Generic Elective Subject will not be available for selection. Students have to apply for other available General Elective subject.

  • The student will loose academic year if failed to apply within stipulated time.
